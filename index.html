Introduction to Fluid Dynamics

By Joseph D. MacMillan

Introduction to Fluid Dynamics is a textbook (and more) aimed at upper undergraduate physics and math students. It covers visualization, the Navier-Stokes equations and solutions to various simple problems, potential theory, the complex potential, aerodynamics, surface waves, and more.
